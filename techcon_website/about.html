History

The Origins and Evolution of TechCon

TechCon began in the early 2010s as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts. The first official event in 2012, known then as “Tech Pioneers United,” brought together developers and innovators to share ideas and build solutions. By 2015, it was rebranded as TechCon, expanding to cover topics like artificial intelligence, hardware integration, and ethical technology use.

Over time, TechCon introduced startup pitch battles, interactive demo zones, and became a platform for underrepresented voices in tech. It went virtual during the pandemic and now embraces a hybrid format.

Mission

TechCon 2024 exists to foster innovation, inclusivity, and future-focused technology solutions. Our mission is to empower the next generation of developers and tech leaders through open collaboration and global networking.

The conference celebrates diversity in ideas, tools, and backgrounds, aiming to create lasting change in the world of technology through education and engagement.

Past Speakers

Radia Perlman

Known as the “Mother of the Internet,” Radia Perlman invented the Spanning Tree Protocol, which is foundational to the operation of network bridges. Her work has shaped modern internet infrastructure.

Dr. Fei-Fei Li

Co-director of the Stanford Human-Centered AI Institute and a pioneer in computer vision and machine learning, Dr. Li has advanced ethical AI development and education worldwide.

Elon Musk

As CEO of companies like Tesla and SpaceX, Musk has revolutionized electric vehicles, space travel, and AI. He spoke at TechCon 2019 about the future of Mars colonization and sustainable energy.
